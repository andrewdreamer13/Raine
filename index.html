Design process

We designed the Raine One from the ground up to be the safest, smoothest, and most exhilarating electric scooter in the world.

With superior acceleration, poly-shock suspension, aircraft grade materials and safety - the Australian designed Raine One is unlike any Scooter you’ve seen before, and the only one you’ll ever need.

Is to give you the freedom to go anywhere, and have fun while you’re doing it. With sustaiable transport, saving you energy and money — owning your journey and loving it.

Performance

The Raine One comes with an advanced flux drive motor, 10 inch high grip all-weather tyres, electronic ABS brakes, and the smoothest throttle for total control in all conditions.

  • Superior design allows a selectable top speed of up to 50kmh / 31mph.

  • The high efficiency motor takes you further and faster.

  • The lightweight frame, coupled with a high powered motor, keeps your ride going through hills and climbs.

Range

With a range of 40km / 25 miles, the Raine One electric scooter will get you where you need to go.

  • Go further with up to 40 kilometres / 25 miles of range.

  • Recharge speed of less than 2 hours for a minimum of 20km / 12.5 miles of range.

  • High grip, soft airless tyres take you where you need to go.
